Pizza

Description

Pizza is a food originating from Italy that is loved worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomata Puree
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Roll the dough into a circle
  2. Spread the tomato puree over the base
  3. Lay the cheese and pepperoni on top
  4. Cook for 15 minutes at gas mark 5
