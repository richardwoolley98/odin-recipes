Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish originating from Italy, composed of pasta sheets and minced meat, usually beef.

Ingredients

  • 6 Pasta Sheets
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 3 Carrots
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown meat and chop carrots
  2. Layer the mix and the pasta sheets
  3. Sprinkle the cheese on top
  4. Cook for 1 hour
